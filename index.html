20/20/20 Rule: Vision Aid

Migranes stink. Screens stink. Additionally, it stinks to have to pay money just to be able to reliably automate the process of maintaining the 20/20/20 rule. I don't like having to purchase a subscription so that I can live my day-to-day as healthy as possible.

To solve this dilema, I designed a PWA that acts as a simple app for tracking my screen use and encouraging me to follow the 20/20/20 rule:

  • 20 minutes of screen use, then
  • 20 seconds looking at something
  • 20 feet away or more
